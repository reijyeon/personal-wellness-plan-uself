PHYSICAL HEALTH

PHYSICAL HEALTH

Keeping my physical body healthy during these times is very essential, I try to drink more water since staying properly hydrated helps the body's cells to function more efficiently. I hope to maintain a morning routine of exercises including doing push-ups, sit-ups, pull-ups among others. Eating healthy foods like fruits and vegetables and less meals with fats. Lastly, seven or eight hours of sleep each night is a must.


MENTAL WELLNESS

MENTAL WELLNESS

Staying positive is the most important thing to do to improve mental health, finding the balance between positive and negative emotions is a must. I personally try to cope with stress through thinking of good memories where I was happy, I also play video games to escape from reality, having a breather or a way to relax would be very helpful. The luxury of having good friends truly makes handling problems much easier, since you know that someone is always there for you and have your back through good and tough times.


EMOTIONAL WELLNESS

EMOTIONAL WELLNESS

I think that the most effective way I could improve my emotional health is express what I feel genuinely, not always suppressing what I feel, since keeping the feeling of sadness or anger can cause problems in my relationships, that's why it's better to let them know what's bothering me, so we could talk it out and clear any misunderstandings, addressing the problem is much better rather than ignoring it in order to reduce emotional stress.


SOCIAL WELLNESS

SOCIAL WELLNESS

Social Interactions are needed by us humans since we are Social Beings. Communicating or connecting with family and friends is important to improve or maintain strong and healthy relationships. I personally try to contact my friends just to ask how they are doing, since it's good to know if they are doing well. If they aren't in a good mood, simply listening to their stories can help. Being there for one another is why we should reach out or stay in touch.


SPIRITUAL WELLNESS

SPIRITUAL WELLNESS

Taking care of ourselves spiritually creates more feeling of harmony and balance within our inner self and the outer world. My plan is to deepen my relationship with myself first, get more clarity on what make me truly happy, what person I want to become and try to live as authentic as possible. After I've focused on myself, I will then make more plans to deepen my connection with others and try to have a positive effect to them.
